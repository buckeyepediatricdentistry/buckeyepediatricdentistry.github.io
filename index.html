Welcome to Buckeye Pediatric Dentistry!  Our practice has been caring for the families of Columbus and the surrounding area since 1979 and we are proud to be a leader in pediatric dentistry. We are glad that you are interested in our practice and we would love to have you join our family. It is our goal to provide your children with healthy teeth and beautiful smiles in a fun and pleasant environment. Our experienced staff is specifically trained and committed to providing the highest quality of dental care for your children.  We are all parents too and understand the needs of both the parents and the patients in our office and strive to treat you like family! We love what we do and we think you will too!

For the same reasons you take your child to the pediatrician, children benefit from seeing a dentist who specializes in the needs specific to children and their teeth. 

Pediatric dentists  complete two extra years of intensive training after they complete dental school.  This training focuses on oral health from infancy through the young adult years. Through this specialized training program, pediatric dentists receive rigorous instruction and clinical experience in management of trauma to baby teeth and young permanent teeth, specialized behavior management, sedation, general anesthesia, space maintenance, infant oral health, dental care for the special needs patient, preventative dentistry and restorative dentistry for children.
