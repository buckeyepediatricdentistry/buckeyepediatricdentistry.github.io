We know that you are busy; our office will make every effort to schedule appointments at your convenience.

Children 5 years old and under will be scheduled in the morning when they are fresher and we can work more slowly with the child for their comfort. School-aged children requiring multiple restorations or extractions should be seen in the morning for the same reason. All children who request or require treatment using nitrous oxide (laughing gas) are also scheduled in the morning. We are happy to provide you with a school excuse for your child’s appointment.

Your scheduled appointment time is reserved exclusively for your child. We ask that you please notify our office 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule the appointment. Another patient who needs our care could be scheduled if we have sufficient time to notify them. We are aware that unforeseen events sometimes require missing an appointment.

Our office is committed to helping you maximize your insurance benefits. We can assist you in estimating your portion of the cost of treatment but due to the variances in insurance policies we cannot guarantee what your insurance will pay. Your estimated portion is due at the time of service. We accept cash, personal checks, debit cards and most major credit cards.

We are happy to file your claim to your insurance company for you and allow them 30 days to render payment. After 60 days you are responsible for the entire balance paid in full .

It is important to know that we file your claim as a courtesy, we do not have a contract with your insurance company. The amount of coverage you have is determined by the type of contract your employer negotiated with the insurance company.

Financing for extensive treatment plans and treatment under general anesthesia is also available.
